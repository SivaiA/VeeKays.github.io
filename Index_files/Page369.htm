About Vee-Kay’s

Welcome to VEE Kays, where tradition meets taste! Established in 2012 in the heart of East London, Eastern Cape, VEE Kays brings you mouth-watering pickles crafted from a long-standing family tradition of preserving flavors and heritage.

Our Story: VEE Kays is more than just a pickle company—it's a celebration of tradition, flavor, and community. Rooted in the rich culinary heritage of the Eastern Cape, our journey began in 2012 with a passion for preserving the vibrant flavors of our region.

Inspired by generations of family recipes and a commitment to quality, VEE Kays has become synonymous with premium pickles made from the freshest ingredients sourced directly from local farms in the Eastern Cape. Every jar of VEE Kays pickle is a labor of love, handcrafted with care and attention to detail to ensure unmatched taste and freshness.

Our Process: At VEE Kays, we believe in keeping it simple and natural. Our pickles are made using traditional methods and fresh ingredients, carefully selected and handpicked from the bountiful farms of the Eastern Cape. From crisp cucumbers to flavorful spices, each component is thoughtfully chosen to deliver the perfect balance of taste and texture in every bite.

Our Commitment: As stewards of tradition and flavor, we take pride in our commitment to quality and authenticity. Our pickles are made without artificial preservatives or additives, ensuring pure, natural goodness in every jar. We believe in transparency and traceability, which is why we work closely with local farmers to source the finest ingredients for our pickles.

Join us on a culinary journey through the flavors of the Eastern Cape with VEE Kays. Whether you're a longtime fan or discovering us for the first time, we invite you to savor the taste of tradition with every bite of VEE Kays pickle.

Thank you for choosing VEE Kays—where tradition meets taste!
